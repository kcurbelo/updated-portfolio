Sketch Mockup

Overview

For this project, I wanted to use Sketch to mock up an app. I wanted to get familiar with how Sketch works and understand the principles and best practices. The versatility and efficiency of Sketch allowed me to quickly mock up designs before finally settling on the final design. I wanted to focus on creating a clean and simple design with not too many bells and whistles since I would be building my knowledge of Sketch. Overall I loved the experience and Sketch is going to be my go-to mockup software in the future.
